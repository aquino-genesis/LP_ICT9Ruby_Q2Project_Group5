Programs

Explore our programs and discover the vibrant community that makes OBMC a unique place for a child to grow and develop.



Casa (Ages 3–5)

This program encourages the transformation of children from ages 3 to 5 as they consciously and willingly learn from their environment.

Primary Grade School (Ages 6–8)

In this program, the classroom’s prepared environment aids the intellectual, physical, and largely independent development of students from ages 6 to 8.

Intermediate Grade School (Ages 9–12)

In this program, the students from ages 9 to 12 deepen their knowledge about their universal responsibility to care for life and the environment.

Junior High School (Ages 13–16)

Our JHS Program supports the emotional, psychological, and social transformation of students from ages 13 to 16 as they prepare for adult responsibilities.

Senior High School (Ages 17–18)

The SHS Program further equips the students ages 17 and 18 with the skills and knowledge necessary for future success.



Campuses
